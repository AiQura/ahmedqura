Learning Outcomes

During this course I learnt:

  • Developing an understanding of the history and future of Artificial Intelligence.
  • Developing an understanding of AI fundamentals including the Alan Turing Test.
  • Examining different learning algorithms usnig WEKA tool.

  • Developing programming abilities for learning algorithm implementation using Python.
